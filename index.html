Research Theme

This R&D aims to unify multiresolution principles/ wavelets/ filter banks/ multi-rate systems/ time-frequency methods with machine learning/ deep learning/ neural networks to analyze one dimensional, two dimensional and higher-dimensional data and make inferences from it.
